About

Nino Godoradze is a data scientist, speaker, and Fulbright Scholar whose work sits at the intersection of psychology, data science, and human-centered innovation.

With a background that bridges psychology, economics, and analytics, she is especially interested in how data can be used to improve human lives. Her work and talks connect technical thinking with real-world outcomes.

This website is a home for her speaking portfolio, selected experiences, and future collaborations with organizers, communities, and teams.
